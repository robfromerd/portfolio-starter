Case Study: A Stack of Questions

The Design Problem

Our nonprofit client felt that a long list of questions in a learning application might overwhelm users, and testing with an early prototype revealed this to be true.

Our Solution

We designed a custom implentation of the Swiper.js library that displays the learning module questions in a friendly, semi-transparent stack that allows students to click or swipe through at their will – without having to scroll through a long list of questions.
